acuMeet is a video conferencing solution by Acupace Technologies Pvt Ltd. It assists masses in the current digital and progressive era, aiding diversity of enterprises to operate smoothly and securely without facing any financial crisis, bridging the communication gap between each entity with its easy accessibility and user-friendly interface.

As we all know due to COVID-19, the various sectors that have been forced to switch to work from home (Remote work) and may be further prefer this working arrangement after normal life continues again. Now, a significant number of enterprises rely on video conferencing solutions. Since Meetings have always been essential to business, school, college, university and government work. Acupace Technologies has developed a simple, powerful, scalable service “acuMeet” that gets out of your way and lets you get to work. Using “acuMeet” you can host/attend online meetings, voice and video chat, along with join conferences, seminars and other video meeting services.
